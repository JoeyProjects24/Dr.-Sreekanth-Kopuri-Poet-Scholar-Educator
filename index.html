About Me

Internationally Acclaimed Poet & Scholar

Dr. Sreekanth Kopuri is an internationally published poet, Pushcart Prize nominee, and scholar whose works have been translated into multiple languages. His poetry explores the intersections of culture, identity, and the human experience.

As an editor, he has worked with prestigious literary journals and anthologies, bringing contemporary voices to the forefront of global literature. His research focuses on postcolonial poetry and the transformative power of language.

A passionate educator, Dr. Kopuri has taught creative writing and literature at universities worldwide, mentoring emerging writers and fostering cross-cultural dialogue through poetry.

"Language is the house of being. In its home man dwells."
- Inspired by Heidegger
